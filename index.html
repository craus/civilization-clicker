People work better when you understand how they do.

Unlocks concept of happiness: the happier your people are, the faster they work.

Your money income is multiplied by (1 + Happiness).

Military

Murder people in other countries, take their possessions!

Different areas provide different bonuses.

Unlock more areas via research.
